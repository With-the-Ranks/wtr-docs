About Spoke Rewired

Spoke Rewired is a political messaging application designed for sending mass-scale peer-to-peer text messages to volunteers and voters. Through the application, you can organize texting campaigns, which provide the structure and tools needed to connect texters in your organization with your organization's contacts. Spoke is meant to be used in combination with a CRM. Spoke is not a CRM and should not be used as one.

Some of Spoke's key features include:
  • Messaging platform with script and survey integration
  • Interaction script and survey editing
  • Automatic and manual conversation assignment
  • Automatic handling and processing of your contact lists
  • Opt-out handling
